Hi, I'm Sandu Subedi

I'm an economic scholar exploring how trade policies, financial access, health policies, and labor markets shape lives in Nepal and beyond. My work combines rigorous econometric and computational methods with real-world policy data to uncover patterns in trade, financial inclusion, labor markets, and public health that shape economic opportunity in Nepal and comparable developing economies.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Business Studies and Master's in Economics from Central Department of Economics, Tribhuvan University. Currently, I work as Economics Instructor at Bridgewater Education Foundation and Research Center.

Research Interests

International Trade | Labor Economics | Health Economics | Agricultural Economics
